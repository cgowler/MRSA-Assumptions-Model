
Code:

model_functions.R: source for ODE model, R0 calculation, and parameter estimation equations

model_assumptions_expanded_test.R: fits different submodel and runs across treatment ranges, producing Fig 2

model_colonized_mult_heatmap.R: runs main model with different colonized LOS and admission rate multipliers to create heatmap in Fig 3

model_colonized_mult_heatmap_homogen.R: runs homogeneous submodel with different colonized LOS and admission rate multipliers to create heatmap in S2_Fig

simultaneous_interventions.R: runs decolonization and transmission reduction interventions simultaneously and plots heatmap results for each submodel, creating S1_Fig



CSV Files:

assumptions_beta_fiting.csv: the parameter values and fitted beta values for each output generated by model_assumptions_expanded_test.R (used in simultaneous_interventions.R and model_assumptions_expanded_test.R)

varying_beta_int.csv: output of results from varying transmission reduction interventions across submodels (from model_assumptions_expanded_test.R) 

varying_decol_int.csv: output of results from varying decolonization interventions across submodels (from model_assumptions_expanded_test.R) 

data_multiplier_casesaverted.csv: output of cases averted results from varying colonization assumptions (LOS and admission rate). Produced by model_colonized_mult_heatmap.R

fit_data.csv: initial fits of beta parameters used in model_colonized_mult_heatmap.R
fit_data.csv: second round of fits of beta parameters used in model_colonized_mult_heatmap.R
fit_data3.csv: final fits of beta parameters used in model_colonized_mult_heatmap.R after refitting

simul_int_data.csv: output of decolonization and transmission reduciton interventions simultaneously (from simultaneous_interventions.R:)
